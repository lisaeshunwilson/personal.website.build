Dr. Lisa Eshun-Wilson

Protein biochemist & structural biologist passionate about health and longevity.

I received my B.S. in Biological Chemistry magna cum laude from Grinnell College and my Ph.D. with Honors from UC Berkeley in the Department of Molecular Cell Biology and the Division of Biochemistry, Biophysics, and Structural Biology. Now, as the founder of Genoma, I am dedicated to advancing gene therapies to cure disease.
